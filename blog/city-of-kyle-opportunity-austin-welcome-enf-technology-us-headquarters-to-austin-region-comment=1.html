City of Kyle, Opportunity Austin welcome ENF Technology's US headquarters to Austin region

City of Kyle, Opportunity Austin welcome ENF Technology's US headquarters to Austin region

Posted By Greater Austin Chamber | Mar 27, 2019
Press Releases, Economic Development, Economic Development Announcements Print Article Share

The City of Kyle and Opportunity Austin, the Greater Austin Chamber's five-county economic development initiative, announced South Korea-based electronic materials manufacturer ENF Technology has chosen to locate its US headquarters in Kyle.

"We are excited to welcome ENF Technology, a high-tech advanced manufacturing company that meets our target markets and our goal of moving forward as an employment hub with high-paying quality jobs," said City of Kyle Director of Economic Development Diana Torres. "The location of ENF in Kyle will also make our community more attractive to additional supply chain companies that could potentially occupy the nearly one million square feet of spec space available and spur development of plentiful greenspace within the City of Kyle."

Established in 2000, ENF Technology is a global leader in electronic materials manufacturing largely used in the semiconductor industry.

ENF Technology plans to build a 125,000-square-foot facility in Kyle's 2,200-acre mixed-use master planned community Plum Creek Development. The company will initially create 40 jobs and eventually expand to 80 employees at this facility.

The City of Kyle worked with Opportunity Austin and representatives from the City of Kyle, Hays County and the Office of the Governor to assist ENF Technology in finding a location for its facility.

“ENF Technology’s decision to locate its US Headquarters in the Plum Creek Development in Kyle is a welcome addition to the area’s burgeoning high-tech manufacturing industry," said Opportunity Austin Senior Vice President of Economic Development Charisse Bodisch. "The symmetry between the company and the region’s existing semiconductor industry reinforces the local supply chain for one of the strongest semiconductor markets in the country.”

The City of Kyle boasts a 700 percent annual growth rate over ten years; 40,000 residential lots are projected to be built within the next 10 to 15 years.

About Opportunity Austin
Opportunity Austin is the economic development initiative aimed at fostering job-creating investment in the five-county Central Texas region. Top priorities include boosting economic diversification to proactively strengthen the economy, deepening the talent pool through development and attraction, and keeping the Greater Austin region attractive to entrepreneurs, business leaders and site selectors through advocacy on issues such as a comprehensive regional transportation system and regional collaboration. Since 2004, Opportunity Austin has helped bring more than 400,000 total jobs to the Austin region. Learn more about Opportunity Austin here.

About ENF Technology
Founded as an electronic materials manufacturer and fine chemical corporation in 2000, ENF Technology Co., Ltd. has been pioneering in the field of electronic materials with the development of new technology and a spirit of challenge. 

About Plum Creek Development
The Plum Creek Development is located in Kyle, 20 miles south of Austin. Upon completion, the 2,200-acre mixed-use master planned community will include 8,700 homes and 700 acres of commercial land, including a 165-acre business park.
