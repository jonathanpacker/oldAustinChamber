Holiday Party Venue Guide

Holiday Party Venue Guide

Posted By Greater Austin Chamber | Dec 03, 2018
Chamber Print Article Share

The holiday season is upon us which means the search for your perfect holiday party venue has commenced. Lucky for you, Austin offers the ideal place regardless of what is on your checklist. From eclectic gems to elegant backdrops, here is a list to get you started on your search.

Cannon + Belle
East 4th Street
Austin, TX 78701

Cannon + Belle prides itself on breaking the rules when it comes to Texan comfort food. Its modern, rustic atmosphere in the heart of downtown Austin is the perfect venue for your holiday party. If you want food, service and a venue that will make your event one for the books, Cannon + Belle is the ideal setting.

Cooper’s Old Time Pit Bar-B-Que
217 Congress Ave
Austin, TX 78701

Cooper’s Old Time Pit Bar-B-Que has the two things needed to make your event happen: party rooms and bar-b-que. Located on the 200 block of South Congress the restaurant is a meat-lover’s oasis in the middle of downtown.

 The Driskill (Driskill Grill)
117 East 7th Street 
Austin, TX 78701

Conveniently located in The Driskill, The Driskill Grill offers an upscale environment for your holiday party. After dinner at the steakhouse, head to The Driskill Bar for cocktails and live music. The timeless rustic atmosphere offered at The Driskill is sure to be a hit with your guests.

The Fairmont Austin
101 Red River
Austin, TX 78701

 As one of Austin’s newest high-rise hotels, the Fairmont Austin is the perfect location for an elegant night featuring some of the best views the city has to offer. Their team of catering professionals will make your night stress-free.

Hotel Van Zandt
605 Davis Street
Austin, TX 78701

Hotel Van Zandt provides both a unique space without sacrificing comfort. The hotel prides itself on its homey, yet creative feel. Make your guests feel at home this holiday party.

JW Marriott
110 East 2nd Street
Austin, TX 78701

Whether your event is intimate or for a whole office, the JW Marriott is the perfect setting for both. Their team of experts are there to help you from start to finish. The hotel’s sophisticated style will add the perfect touch of elegance to your night.

Max’s Wine Dive
207 San Jacinto Boulevard
Austin, TX 78701

Max’s Wine Dive provides a truly unique atmosphere. The Underground features antique finishes and art from Austin artists. Not only is the setting perfect for an event, but guests will be treated to Max’s gourmet comfort food.

Moonshine Patio Bar & Grill
303 Red River
Austin, TX 78701

If you want a true Austin experience, look no further. Moonshine Patio Bar & Grill offers an American cuisine with special touches unique to the restaurant. Host your event on their patio, or inside for a smaller crowd. Enjoy the downtown feel without losing the Austin charm.

The Salt Lick BBQ
18300 FM 1826
Driftwood, TX 78619

An Austin area favorite, The Salt Lick is a guaranteed crowd pleaser. Located in Driftwood, the BBQ and landscape are worth the drive out of town. Even better, the Saltlick Cellars are located next door so treat your party to local wine.”
