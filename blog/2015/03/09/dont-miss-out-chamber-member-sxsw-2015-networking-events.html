Don’t miss out! Chamber member SXSW 2015 networking events.

Don’t miss out! Chamber member SXSW 2015 networking events.

Posted By Greater Austin Chamber | Mar 09, 2015
Chamber Print Article Share

 

 

Data Foundry and Golden Frog are hosting their 3rd annual “Take Back Your Internet” panel discussion and networking party during SXSW. Their panel will focus on all the issues, challenges and loopholes regarding Net Neutrality and the Open Internet.

The Panel

Gigi Sohn, Special Council for External Affairs, FCC

Marvin Ammori, Ammori Group

Chip Pickering, CEO of Comptel

Edward Henigin, CTO of Data Foundry

James Waterworth, vice president of CCIA Europe. 

Event Information:

WHAT: Take Back Your Internet Party – 2015

WHEN: Monday, March 16, 6 pm -12 am

WHERE: The Palm Door, Palm Door on Sixth Street, 508 East 6th Street

COST: No cost to you – just RSVP for the event while tickets last

(Note: This is an unofficial SXSW event, and a badge is not required to attend.)

The Schedule:

6:00 pm: Doors Open

6:00 – 7:00 pm:  Networking

7:00 – 8:30 pm: Expert Panel on Open Internet

8:30 pm – 12:00 am:  Music, Party, Networking

RSVP: http://www.goldenfrog.com/sxsw2015

Don’t miss out on the opportunity to learn new skills from experienced practitioners!

COST: Free, no badge necessary

The University of Texas at Austin will host their first ever UT Village at SXSW Interactive 2015.  The event will showcase UT’s world class research, innovation, and entrepreneurial programs. The UT Village panel sessions and Innovation Showcase will focus on a variety of topics, ranging from healthcare devices and 3-D printing to connected cars and augmented reality tech.  Attendees will have the opportunity to explore the research, network with the creators, get a behind-the-scenes look at the latest technologies coming out of UT Austin, and enjoy a networking reception to wrap up the day.

Event Information:

WHAT: UT Village @ SXSW Interactive

WHEN:  Sunday, March 15 9:30am – 6:00pm

WHERE: Radisson Hotel & Suites Austin Downtown Ballroom – 111 E Cesar Chavez St., 78701

COST:  Open to all SXSW Interactive, Platinum, and Gold badge holders

Schedule:

9:30-10:30am: Digital Austin: 3D Maps That Predict What’s Ahead

11am – noon: 3D Printing Becomes Additive Manufacturing

12:30-1:30pm: Connected Vehicles: Challenges and Possibilities

2-3pm: UT Austin National Science Foundation I-Corps Node

3:30-4:30 pm: Making Big Data More Digestible

4:30-6pm: Innovation Showcase and Reception: Visit with researches and view interactive exhibits
