Austin Chamber announces Austin Gives Generous Business Awards keynote speaker, finalists

Austin Chamber announces Austin Gives Generous Business Awards keynote speaker, finalists

Posted By Greater Austin Chamber | Mar 05, 2019
Press Releases Print Article Share

The Austin Gives Generous Business Awards luncheon will be held at the Hilton Austin on Apr. 12.

 

AUSTIN, Texas—The Austin Chamber of Commerce today announced HEB Central Texas Region Senior Vice President and General Manager Jeff Thomas will receive the Bobby Jenkins Philanthropic Leadership Award and keynote the Austin Gives Generous Business Awards on Apr. 12.

As the Philanthropic Leadership Award honoree, Thomas will share how he came to embrace, lead and cultivate the next generation of leaders while maintaining the commitment and culture set forth by the founders of HEB. 

The Chamber also announced the Generous Business Awards finalists:

One winner will be named in each category.

“Austin is incredibly fortunate to have an outstanding business culture," said Austin Chamber Senior Director of Engagement Beth Krueger. "Austin Gives looks to honor all of the job creators and employers that make giving back to the community a part of their business model. Each year special recognition is given to those businesses that are giving back in meaningful and impactful ways. We receive a multitude of deserving nominees and are confident Austin will once again bring forth the best stories of how businesses are making a difference through their philanthropy.”

Austin Gives recognizes businesses that have pledged 1 percent of pre-tax earnings—such as volunteer time, products, services and financial support—toward community impact efforts. The program is voluntary with no cost to participants.

Tickets and tables for the Generous Business Awards luncheon can be purchased at https://members.austinchamber.com/events/austin-gives-generous-business-awards.

Media is invited to attend but must RSVP to dtrevino@austinchamber.com.

ABOUT AUSTIN GIVES
The Austin Gives Award program was created to recognize Austin businesses for their efforts to give back to community-based, non-profit organizations. Since its launch in 2011, the program has celebrated Austin as one of the most philanthropic regions in the nation. Learn more at austin.gives.
