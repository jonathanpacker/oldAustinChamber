Flawless lashes on a modest budget

Flawless lashes on a modest budget

Posted By Greater Austin Chamber | Oct 25, 2017
Membership Print Article Share

Amazing Lash Studio Hancock opens upscale studio in Austin 


Amazing Lash Studio Hancock celebrated the opening of its location in Austin with a grand ribbon cutting. A first-year member of the Greater Austin Chamber, attendees enjoyed hors-d’oeuvres and all things eye candy.

“Giving women the confidence to feel good at a reasonable price is what we are about,” said Cassandra Ghaffar, Studio Owner of Amazing Lash Studio Hancock. “Our team is dedicated to providing exemplary customer service and helping women discover their beauty with eyelash extensions.”

Located in the HEB Hancock Center (1000 E. 41st Street in Suite 200A), Amazing Lash Studio Hancock is dedicated to offering Austinites natural-looking eyelash extensions at an affordable price. Customers can choose between four lash styles—natural, cute, sexy, and gorgeous—to create their perfect look in an easy process. Each strand is applied one-by-one to your real eyelashes creating a fuller look. Since the lash extensions are low in maintenance, you don’t have to worry about glues or eyelash curlers. Additionally, the extensions are waterproof and will last thorough showers, baths, pools, or beach vacations.

All eyelash specialists are licensed cosmetologist and estheticians who are devoted exclusively to the art of lash beauty. Patrons can enjoy their grand special sale of an initial set of eyelashes for $54.99 until November 30.

The ribbon cutting was held October 24. For more information on Amazing Lash Studio Hancock, visit their website here.

If you are a Chamber member and would like to coordinate a ribbon-cutting ceremony, contact Lacy Roesler at 512-322-5613.
