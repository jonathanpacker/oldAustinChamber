New Member Spotlight: Infinite Fountain Productions

New Member Spotlight: Infinite Fountain Productions

Posted By Greater Austin Chamber | Jul 27, 2017
Membership Print Article Share

Why did you join the Austin Chamber?
We joined the Chamber to meet people behind the businesses which have made Austin such a wonderful place to live.

In 150 words or less, what is Infinite Fountain Productions?
Infinite Fountain Productions is a high-end video production company. We are veteran film makers that provide our services to companies looking for cinematic quality productions. We offer the highest quality video production with the best equipment and crew available. We also have proprietary technology that allows for voice active videos. Our voice active videos can be used for websites that visitors and clients can interact with for increased viewer engagement. 

Why did your company decide to operate in Austin?
As the CEO, I have spent most of my life here in Austin since 1988. I fell in love with the city and decided to make it my home. Having spent a stint in San Francisco and New York, where I was able to hone my craft, I always returned home to Austin.  

My favorite thing about the Austin Chamber is…
the opportunity to engage with long-established and new up and coming businesses here in Austin.

What do you hope to gain from your Austin Chamber membership?
As a start-up company, we hope to meet people that will be interested in what we have to offer and an opportunity to learn and improve our products according to their needs.

What do you want the world to know about your company’s commitment to the Central Texas region?
Austin is home to multiple companies that started in garages and ended up on the Fortune 500 list. We want to show the world how beautiful and wonderful Austin is through corporate and narrative videos. 

Is there anything else you would like to share with us?
We would love the opportunity to shoot high-quality videos in and around Austin as much as possible to share with the world. For more information, visit our website here.

Greater Austin Chamber

The Greater Austin Chamber of Commerce focuses on how the Austin region works. We serve as the voice of business for our member organizations and their employees throughout Central Texas. Our mission is to provide leadership that facilitates the creation of a prosperous regional economy and effective advocacy for members.
