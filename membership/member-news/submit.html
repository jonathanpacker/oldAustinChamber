Chamber Member News

Member News allows you to share information with other members, Central Texas and beyond! To submit your Member News please fill out the form below.

Submit Your Member News

For Austin Chamber members only. Submissions must be submitted by noon on Friday to be considered for the following week. Submissions will run for seven days. Submissions are limited to two per week, totaling 100 words.

Please note:
Duplicate text submissions must be submitted at least two weeks apart. We do not run duplicate submissions two weeks in a row, and re-submissions are only considered after all new memeber news has been posted.
