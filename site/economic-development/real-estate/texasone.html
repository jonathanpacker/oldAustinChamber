Texas 1 (Data Center)

The Austin region has several unique larger real estate opportunities either existing or delivering soon.

Featured Existing Buildings

Texas 1 (Data Center)
4100 Smith School Road
Austin, TX 78744

Data Foundry owns and operates three Austin data centers: Texas 1, Texas 2 and Austin 1.  Texas 1 and Austin 1 are completed, while Texas 2 was recently completed.  The Austin data centers feature carrier-netural connectivity, multi-layered security and a variety of managed services with 24X7X365 support.

Texas 1 is a 130,000 square foot purpose-built data center. This robust facility was the first of its kind in Central Texas and has a structural wind rating of 215 mph. Texas 1 can accommodate a variety of deployment types, including High Performance Computing (HPC) solutions with up to 50 kW per cabinet.  It featured dual power feed, 16+ network carrier options, chilled water cooling and 8 layers of security.

Texas 2 was completed in 2018 and is their third data center in Austin. In its final phase, Texas 2 will be a 325,000 square foot, 36 MW data center. The facility will support high performance computing, dedicated office space for colocation and office for disaster recovery.

Data Foundry has been providing data center outsourcing, colocation and disaster recovery services since 1994, supporting thousands of customers across a wide variety of businesses including energy, financial services, healthcare, high performance computing firms, as well as hosting service providers and technology companies.
