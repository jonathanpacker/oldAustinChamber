  • Featured Office Real Estate

This page highlights a variety of the area's excellent larger real estate opportunities that include office or flex/value office. Featured existing buildings are scheduled to deliver in the first half of 2018, while featured upcoming projects have a later expected delivery date.

When you're ready for personalized, professional site selection assistance covering all the options, please contact Charisse Bodisch, CEcD, Senior Vice President, Economic Development (512.322.5608). If you would like identify local real estate professionals and other prospective business partners please see Opportunity Austin's corporate partners on our Opportunity Austin Investors page.

Featured Existing Buildings - Office

901 E 5th St (Plaza Saltillo)

901 E 5th Street is a new Class A office building in East Austin at the Plaza Saltillo development that includes 150,000 SF of space delivering in June 2019. This unique 8-story project features a typical floor plan of 21,942 SF with 14 -foot floor to ceiling glass on all levels. The building is parked at a ratio of 2.7:1,000 SF in garage parking with additional parking available to supplement.  Floors 5-8 feature downtown views. The Saltillo development also features 115,000 SF of retail and restaurants including Whole Foods as well as over 800 residential units.
View Full Description »

Brushy Creek Corporate Center

Brushy Creek Corporate Center is an excellent opportunity in Cedar Park with easy access to TX-45 and US 183.  Located on 16 acres the two building campus is 231,030 SF and is a great opportunity for either value office or flex space.  Each building has a 24 foot clear height with 50 foot by 40 foot column spacing and dock high doors.  Users may be able to access incentive opportunities through Cedar Park.  The Brushy Creek buildings delivered in August 2018.
View Full Description »

MetCenter Creative Office

MetCenter has a variety of existing buildings and buildings currently under construction, as well as approved site and design plans for additional buildings. With all infrastructure already in place, MetCenter is specifically tailored for flexibility in type and cost of delivery of various product types, as well as the fastest speed of delivery possible.  Currently under development is the creative office portion of the campus that will consist of up to 4 buildings and 400K SF of space and include features such as generous amounts of natural light, roll-up glass overhead doors and plenty of interactive space. 
View Full Description »

Mira Vista

Mira Vista is a 121,147 SF Class A office building in Austin's Southwest submarket.  The building offers excellent access via Mopac Expressway and Bee Caves Road and is less than five minutes from downtown.  The site offers extensive Austin skyline views and is within walking distance to restaurants and retail. 
View Full Description »

Parmer 3.4

Parmer Austin is a premier business park that includes hundreds of thousands of square feet of newly developed office and industrial space that has attracted current tenants such as Home Depot, Blue Apron, Natera and more.  Parmer 3.4 is the next addition to the park adding 115,000 SF of high quality office in a four story building.  The site features a high parking ratio of 5.25/1,000 SF. Parmer features amenities such as a hike & bike trail, outdoor grove for recreation, and over 2 million SF of retail and restaurants in close proximity.
View Full Description »

Preserve at 620

Preserve at 620 offers cutting edge, modern office space overlooking the Balcones Canyonlands, just minutes from Lake Travis. Completion of the 226,000 SF office building happened in January 2019.  Amenity rich, flooding with natural light, uniquely efficient and eminently scalable, Preserve at 620 delivers unmatched value to your business and your people. Ample parking facilitates modern densities and in-place growth.
View Full Description »

Summit II

Summit II is a 95,000 SF Class A office project in Round Rock located near La Frontera.  The building is two stories and features large floor plates of 45,000 SF.  Amenities include a 10,000 SF landscaped courtyard, on-site gym and shower, and a high parking ratio of 4.7:1,000 SF.  Summit II is located in close proximity to major thoroughfares including I-35, SH-45, and Mopac.  Completion of the building occurred in June 2018.
View Full Description »

Texas 1 (Data Center)

Data Foundry owns and operates three Austin data centers: Texas 1, Texas 2 and Austin 1.  Texas 1 and Austin 1 are completed, while Texas 2 was recently finished.  The Austin data centers feature carrier-netural connectivity, multi-layered security and a variety of managed services with 24X7X365 support. Austin 1 is a 40K SF data center, while Texas 1 consists of 130K SF and Texas 2 is 325K SF of data center space on 40 acres.
View Full Description »

Featured Upcoming Projects - Office

Centerpark San Marcos

Centerpark is a best-in-class business park planned for fast-growing San Marcos that will bring nearly 2.4 million SF of industrial/distribution space in 8 buildings and 240,000 SF of office in 3 buildings. Centerpark's close proximity to I-35 gives it access to both the Austin and San Antonio metro areas.  The site is located within a competitive tax and regulatory environment and within a Triple Freeport Zone. Site plans include flexibility for build-to-suit.
View Full Description »

The District

The District is a large scale mixed use development featuring office, retail, and residential slated to begin construction in 2018 in Round Rock.  The 65 acre development is located close to SH 45 and I 35 will deliver up to 3 million SF of office, over 200,000 SF of retail, over 900 multifamily units, and a contemporary upscale hotel.  Designed to be pedestrian-friendly, plans call for plenty of open space and a future planned transit station.
View Full Description »

The Foundry

Foundry II is a dynamic workspace under construction in East Austin that will bring 161,000 SF of creative workspace into the market by Summer 2020.  The six story office features ground floor offices with double height entrances, four levels of below grade parking, and spacious, private terraces on each floor.  This Cielo developed property will seek LEED Silver designation.
View Full Description »

The Grove

The Grove is an exciting mixed-use development coming to Central Austin in Q1 2020.  The development spans 75 acres and includes 179K SF of Class A office space, luxury and sustainable homes, responsible local retailers and all daily conveniences of a flourishing neighborhood.  A 16 acre signature park is featured on the property as well as 20+ acres of green space, upscale homes, specialty retail, gourmet cafes, creative work spaces and versatile corporate offices.
View Full Description »

Highland

Highland is a new energetic, walkable, and transit-friendly mixed use neighborhood that is emerging from the former Highland Mall.  The development includes plans for up to 1.1M SF of Class A office, 1,320 apartment units, a 1.3M SF Austin Community College Campus and 100K SF of retail.  The office portion of the development includes 4 buildings with Highland 1 which includes 500K SF in 4 floors beginning construction in March 2019. 
View Full Description »

Terawest

Terawest is an 88 acre mixed-use development that will bring up to 500,000 SF of Class A to the northern portion of the region with availability starting as early as 2020 Q2.  The property is located in the City of Georgetown at Westinghouse Road and Mays Drive with great access to University Blvd., I-35 and SH 130.  The office buildings will feature efficient floor plates and a high parking ratio of up to 6:1,000.
View Full Description »
