This calculator should be used only as a guide in calculating potential FTZ benefits. It provides only gross savings estimates, and does not take into account FTZ operations. Typically, companies with FTZ gross savings of over $100,000 may find the FTZ an attractive option.

We encourage companies to conduct a formal cost benefit analysis to arrive at a more accurate savings number. There may be additional savings opportunities not addressed by the above calculator. The cost benefit analysis will also allow a company to evaluate any operational impact of the FTZ on the company's current operations.
