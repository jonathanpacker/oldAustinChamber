Austin Chamber announces finalists for 2019 Greater Austin Business Awards

Austin Chamber announces finalists for 2019 Greater Austin Business Awards

Posted By Greater Austin Chamber | Jun 20, 2019
Membership Print Article Share

The Austin Chamber today announced finalists for the 2019 Greater Austin Business Awards.

Now in its 19th year, the program celebrates excellence in the local business community which helps make the Austin region one of the most desirable places in the world to live, work, and play.

The finalists were selected by a panel of independent judges. Award categories include Community Relations, Customer Experience, Employee Health & Wellness, Environmental Champion, Innovation, Consumer Product, Executive Leader, Company Culture, International Reach, First-Time Entrepreneur, Non-Profit, and Uniquely Austin.
