AUSTIN JOB POSTINGS REPORT JANUARY 2018

AUSTIN - ROUND ROCK - SAN MARCOS MSA
(DECEMBER 14, 2017 to JANUARY 13, 2018)
austinchamber.com/jobpostings

(NOT FOR CITATION OR CIRCULATION WITHOUT PERMISSION)

INSIGHTS

  • About 59 percent of all Available Job Postings for 2017 required some form of postsecondary education as the level of education needed for entry into the job posted.  

  • 35,500 Available Job Postings during January 2018, about 740 less postings than January 2017. Office and Administrative Support Occupations saw a 670 decrease in year-to-year Available Job Postings, followed by Computer and Mathematical Occupations which had 340 decrease. Available Job Postings in January has historically been lower than other months of the year, followed by December.

  • Education, Training and Library Occupations continues to see an increase of year-to-year postings by about 280, mostly for Teacher Assistants, Preschool Teachers, Elementary School Teachers, Secondary School Teachers and Special Education Teachers. This is followed by Healthcare Practitioners and Technical Occupations which saw a 260 year-to-year increase, mostly for Registered Nurses, Licensed Practical and Licensed Vocational Nurses and Speech-Language Pathologists.

  • Microsoft Office is a hard skill tracked in this most recent report and is an important hard skill to acquire based on the demand seen.

  • The share of New Job Postings compared to Available Job Postings is approximately 41 percent in January 2018. 

  • According to the Texas Workforce Commission, the Austin MSA reported an unemployment rate of 2.7% or 30,800 unemployed in December 2017, lower than the 3.2% or 35,500 back in December 2016. 

  • In January 2017, 45 percent of Available Job Postings were Less than 30 days old. In January 2018, only 39 percent of Available Job Postings are Less than 30 days old, meaning that employers are having to post jobs for a longer period of time.

  • The level of hiring difficulty for Software Developers, Applications is the same as Registered Nurses.

  • More jobs ahead. Companies recently announced new location or expansion plans in the Austin MSA, representing about 409 plus jobs. The expansion list includes Hyliion (Pittsburgh, PA), LKQ, Parsley Energy, Silicon Laboratories, TrendKite and WeWork.

The following job market report is designed to provide insight into current employer demands, skills and occupations in the Austin MSA.

There were approximately 35,500 Available Job Postings during the January 2018 monthly reporting period, about 740 less postings when compared to January 2017. 

About 5,900 or 17 percent of the Available Job Postings during the January reporting period fell under Computer and Mathematical Occupations, which also saw the largest decline in year-to-year Available Job Postings. 

The Major Occupational Groups that saw the largest increases in the volume of Available Job Postings, when compared to January 2017, are Education, Training and Library Occupations and Healthcare Practitioners and Technical Occupations. 41 percent or 14,500 are New Job Postings.

According to the Texas Workforce Commission, the Austin MSA reported an unemployment rate of 2.7% or 30,800 unemployed in December 2017, lower than the 3.2% or 35,500 back in December 2016. Approximately 63% of unemployment insurance (UI) claimants that are actively seeking a job in Travis County have less than an associate's degree, according to Workforce Solutions Capital Area. According to the American Community Survey 2011-2015, there are 76,142 employed individuals living below the poverty line and 99,296 families living at less than 200 percent of poverty.

In January 2017, 45 percent of Available Job Postings were Less than 30 days old. In January 2018, only 39 percent of Available Job Postings are Less than 30 days old, meaning that employers are having to posting jobs for a longer period of time.

TOP 20 JOB POSTINGS

The chart below lists the top 20 unique available job postings in the Austin – Round Rock – San Marcos MSA from December 14, 2017 to January 13, 2018.Source: The Conference Board Help Wanted OnLine ® (HWOL) and Bureau of Labor Statistics, May 2016

Registered Nurses and Retail Salespersons both saw a 100 or more increase in year-to-year postings. Office Clerks, General, Customer Service Representatives, Laborers and Freight, Stock, and Material Movers, Hand and Network and Computer Systems Administrators had a 100 or more decrease in year-to-year postings.

TOP 20 JOB POSTINGS AND HIRING DIFFICULTY

The chart below lists the top 20 unique available job postings and its hiring difficulty in the Austin – Round Rock – San Marcos MSA from December 14, 2017 to January 13, 2018.Source: Wanted Technologies and the Bureau of Labor Statistics

The Hiring Scale views the difficulty-to-fill indicator on market conditions. It calculates a score for each position that the employer is trying to fill. Several factors affect the hiring scale score that include the supply of available candidates, the overall demand for employers for people in the position, the location, the occupation, local unemployment rates, job site source, offered pay level, etc. The Hiring Score scores jobs from 1 to 99 based on their projected difficulty-to-recruit.

Half of the Top 20 occupations posted, or about 5,700 postings, require less than an associate's degree as the typical education needed for entry. Retail Salespersons and Maids and Housekeeping Cleaners posted an annual median wage of less than $30,000 in the Austin MSA. First-Line positions in retail, food preparation and office administration do pay an annual median wage of close to $40,000 and above, it would also require some years of related work experience. First-Line Supervisors of Retail Sales Workers is the hardest to fill in the First-Line positions.

TOP 20 TECH JOB POSTINGS

The chart below lists the top 20 unique available job postings under Computer and Mathematical Occupations in the Austin – Round Rock – San Marcos MSA from December 14, 2017 to January 13, 2018.Source: The Conference Board Help Wanted OnLine ® (HWOL) and Bureau of Labor Statistics, May 2016

Information Technology Project Managers and Computer and Information Research Scientists and Software Developers, Applications saw the largest year-to-year increase in Available Job Postings. 

Network and Computer Systems Administrators, Web Developers and Computer Systems Analysts saw the largest decline in year-to-year Available Job Postings.

Companies such as Apple, Inc., Merck, Booz Allen Hamilton, Home Depot and General Electric posted 40 or more year-to-year increase in postings under this category. 

Compared nationwide, the Austin-Round Rock-San Marcos MSA has a lower hiring difficulty for Computer and Mathematical Occupations. The Washington-Arlington-Alexandria MSA, Atlanta-Sandy Springs-Roswell MSA, Dallas-Fort Worth-Arlington MSA, Boston-Cambridge-Newton MSA, Chicago-Naperville-Elgin MSA, Philadelphia-Camden-Wilmington MSA and the Denver-Aurora-Lakewood MSA have a higher hiring difficulty in this occupational category when compared to the Austin-Round Rock-San Marcos MSA.
