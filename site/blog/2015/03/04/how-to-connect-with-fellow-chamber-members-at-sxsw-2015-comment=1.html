How to Connect with Fellow Chamber Members at SXSW 2015

How to Connect with Fellow Chamber Members at SXSW 2015

Posted By Greater Austin Chamber | Mar 04, 2015
Chamber, Membership Print Article Share

SXSW is just around the corner! At SXSW Interactive, don’t miss out on the Trade Show. It hosts an assorted range of innovative exhibitors from startups, to well-known industry leaders. The four-day event is a great way to make connections and learn about the novelties of the past year. We also have several members participating in panels/sessions at SXSW Interactive. Make sure to stop by and support Chamber members!

SXSW Trade Show

When: March 15-18, 10:00 a.m. – 6:00 p.m. (closes 2 p.m. on March 18)

Where: Austin Convention Center, Exhibit Halls 3 & 4

Member Exhibitors:

  • Capital One
  • Enterprise Ireland
  • Freescale Semiconductor, Inc.,
  • Grande Communications
  • Hewlett-Packard
  • IBM
  • Meltwater Group
  • Pearson
  • Powerhouse Animation
  • Q2ebanking
  • ShipStation (Auctane, LLC)
  • SoftServe
  • Sony / Omega Broadcast (Sony Online Entertainment)
  • Tableau
  • Unanet
  • United States Postal Service
  • UT McCombs School of Business – MBA Program
  • WP Engine

SXSW Interactive Panels and Sessions

When: March 13-17, Sessions throughout the day

Where: Various locations

Member Panels:

  • Making to Manufacturing 

           When:  March 13 3:30 – 4:30 p. m.

           Where: Long Center, West Pincer Lounge, 701 w Riverside Dr

  • UI? UX? Visual Designer? Let’s Settle This

           When: March 14 11:00 a.m. – 12:00 p.m.

           Where: JW Marriott, Room 407, 110 E 2nd Street

  • Unicorn #9: How Entrepreneurs Choose the Next Idea

           When: March 13 5:00 – 6:00 p.m.

           Where: Hilton Austin Downtown, Salon G, 500 E 4th Street

  • Can Anyone Really Trust User-Generated Content?

           When: March 14 12:30 – 1:30 p.m.

           Where: Hyatt Regency Austin, Texas Ballroom 4-7, 208 Barton Springs Rd

  • Designing Austin’s Economy – An Innovation Uproar?

           When: March 15 11:00 a.m. – 12:00 p.m.

           Where: Hilton Austin Downtown, Room 400-402, 500 E 4th Street

  • Put IBM Watson to Work and Transform Your Business

           When: March 16 2:00 – 3:00 p.m.

           Where: Radisson Town Lake, Riverside Ballroom, 111 E Cesar Chavez

This SXSW is sure to be a great one — don’t miss out on SXSW Interactive Director Hugh Forrest’s blog post on the top 10 trends this year.

Greater Austin Chamber

The Greater Austin Chamber of Commerce focuses on how the Austin region works. We serve as the voice of business for our member organizations and their employees throughout Central Texas. Our mission is to provide leadership that facilitates the creation of a prosperous regional economy and effective advocacy for members.
