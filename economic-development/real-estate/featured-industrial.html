  • Featured Industrial Real Estate

This page highlights a variety of the area's excellent larger real estate opportunities that include flex, warehouse or industrial product. Featured existing buildings are scheduled to deliver in the first half of 2018, while featured upcoming projects have a later expected delivery date.

When you're ready for personalized, professional site selection assistance covering all the options, please contact Charisse Bodisch, CEcD, Senior Vice President, Economic Development (512.322.5608). If you would like identify local real estate professionals and other prospective business partners please see Opportunity Austin's corporate partners on our Opportunity Austin Investors page.

Featured Existing Buildings - Industrial

Brushy Creek Corporate Center

Brushy Creek Corporate Center is an excellent opportunity in Cedar Park with easy access to TX-45 and US 183.  Located on 16 acres the two building campus is 231,030 SF and is a great opportunity for either value office or flex space.  Each building has a 24 foot clear height with 50 foot by 40 foot column spacing and dock high doors.  Users may be able to access incentive opportunities through Cedar Park.  Brushy Creek will deliver in August 2018.
View Full Description »

Buda Park 35

Buda's Park 35 is a new 305,768 SF industrial building under construction in Buda with estimated availability in January 2019. The building features convenient access to I-35 and TX-45 tollway. Economic incentives and fast track permitting is available as well as triple freeport tax exemption. Park 35 has a clear height of 32 feet and features 76 dock doors and 4 ramp doors and will be divisible to 60,000 SF.
View Full Description »

Hays Logistics Center

The Hays Logistics Center is primed for large e-commerce distributors looking to directly service over 4 million people in the Austin-San Antonioreigon. The development includes over 428,000 SF across two buildings both featuring cross-dock loading and 32 foot clear height.  Building 1 is under construction and will deliver around Q1 of 2019, while building 2 should begin construction in 2019.
View Full Description »

Hutto Innovation Business Park

The Hutto Innovation Business Park consists of 870,000 SF of planned office and industrial space with room for expansion on 72-acres of land near the East Williamson County Education Center.  The project is being constructed in two phases with the first phase including a 150,000 SF spec industrial building available in Spring 2019.  Upon build out, the development will consist of 6 total buildings.
View Full Description »

Settlers Crossing

Settlers Crossing is a two-phased, four-building industrial park in Round Rock that will feature tilt-wall construction with ample storefront entries allowing for flexible suite sizes.  The first two buildings are ready for occupancy as of December 2018 and the remaining two buildings are under construction in 2019. Each building will feature dock-high and grade-level loading and will vary in clear height from 28 feet to 30 feet.  The site is triple freeport exempt and a variety of additional incentives may be available for tenants.  
View Full Description »

Featured Upcoming Projects - Industrial

Bastrop Business and Industrial Park

Bastrop’s Business and Industrial Park is 263 acres at the juncture of State Highways 71, 95, and 21 in Bastrop - just 25 minutes southeast of Austin. The Park is home to a mix of enterprises including a medical manufacturer, a drug testing facility, and a major art sculpture foundry, among others. The Bastrop EDC manages the Park and can offer some enticing incentives for primary employers.
View Full Description »

Centerpark San Marcos

Centerpark is a best-in-class business park planned for fast-growing San Marcos that will bring nearly 2.4 million SF of industrial/distribution space in 8 buildings and 240,000 SF of office in 3 buildings. Centerpark's close proximity to I-35 gives it access to both the Austin and San Antonio metro areas.  The site is located within a competitive tax and regulatory environment and within a Triple Freeport Zone. Site plans include flexibility for build-to-suit.
View Full Description »

Heatherwilde 45

Heatherwilde 45 is a 27 acre industrial park that will deliver approximately 360,000 SF of Class A industrial space to the North submarket in Pflugerville.  Plans consist of an initial phase of three buildings two with 28' clear height and one with 24'.  The property is located off the frontage road of SH 45 which provides excellent connectivity to SH 130, I-35 and Mopac. Construction is expected to begin in September 2018.
View Full Description »

Hutto MegaSite

The City of Hutto MegaSite provides over 1,480 acres, with approximately 98% of the property buildable. Infrastructure is ready at or near the site. Electicity is deregulated.  There is great access via US 79 and SH 130. The MegaSite is minutes from Interstate 35 as well. Dual class rail by both BNSF and Union Pacific is available.  Several airports are nearby including Austin-Bergstrom airport.The MegaSite is perfect for a multi-use business, technology and/or industrial park as well as company headquarters. 
View Full Description »

Kyle Crossing Business Park

The Kyle Crossing Business Park is a 40 acre multi-tenant business park planned in Kyle that is set to begin construction in September 2019.  Currently planned are two buildings consisting of a combined 535,000 SF of industrial/flex warehouse.  Building One is just over 227K SF and features 32 foot clear height and is divisible to 20K SF, while Building Two is just over 307K SF with a clear height of 36 feet and is divisible to 50K SF. 
View Full Description »
