The Austin region has several unique larger real estate opportunities either existing or delivering soon.

Featured Upcoming Projects

Heatherwilde 45
SH 45 & Heatherwilde Blvd.
Pflugerville, TX 78660

Heatherwilde 45 is a 27 acre industrial park that will deliver approximately 360,000 SF of Class A industrial space to the Northeast submarket within the City of Pflugerville.  Plans consist of an initial phase of three buildings two with 28' clear height and one with 24'.  The property is located off the frontage road of SH 45 which provides excellent connectivity to SH 130, I-35 and Mopac. Construction is expected to begin in September 2018.
