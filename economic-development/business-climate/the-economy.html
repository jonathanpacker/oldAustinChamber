The Austin Economy

Economic Indicators is a numbers update on select indicators in the Central Texas region that chronicle economic activity on a monthly or quarterly basis. The report and associated Excel files are posted on or about the first Wednesday of the month.

July 2019 Economic Indicators
  • pdf This month's 8-page economic indicators report.
  • pdf Also available in a slide deck format.

The set of slides above is updated irregularly. The current version represents data through early 2019. However, each of the Excel files below include up-to-date graphs of many of the same indicators.
If you do not have Adobe Acrobat Reader installed on your computer, you can download it here for free.

Excel Files for Downloading

Note that most of these Excel files contain multiple sheets (see tabs at the bottom of spreadsheet window).
